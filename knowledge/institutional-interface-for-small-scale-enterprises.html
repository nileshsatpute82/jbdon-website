Small enterprises benefit from a network of institutional support that provides resources and opportunities.

Government Agencies offer registration assistance, subsidies, training, and market access programs.

Financial Institutions provide capital through development banks, microfinance, and guarantee schemes.

Industry Associations offer networking, advocacy, and shared services for members.

Business Development Organizations provide incubation, training, and consulting services.
