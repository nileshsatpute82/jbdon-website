Market structure fundamentally shapes pricing power and strategy.

Pure Competition features many sellers with identical products—firms are price takers.

Competitive Dynamics drive profits toward zero through entry and exit.

Pure Monopoly has a single seller facing the entire market demand curve.

Monopoly Pricing sets price where marginal revenue equals marginal cost, producing less at higher prices.
