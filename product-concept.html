A product concept translates a raw idea into a defined offering before full development.

From Idea to Concept adds definition—target customer, benefits, form, positioning.

Concept Components include target market, customer need, key benefits, and reason to believe.

Concept Testing validates appeal with potential customers through statements and feedback.

Concept-Product Fit ensures developed products deliver on the concept promise.
