Survey research systematically collects information to generate actionable insights.

Research Objectives must be clear before design—what decisions will the survey inform?

Survey Design creates clear, unbiased questions with appropriate formats.

Sampling Strategy determines who responds—random for inference, convenience for speed.

Response Quality addresses non-response bias and survey fatigue issues.
